The Magic of Chiptune

The CodeDash! soundtrack is crafted in "chiptune" style, a music genre that uses the sound chips of old computers and consoles (like the Commodore 64 or Game Boy). Every note and effect has been meticulously programmed using a "tracker," software that allows composing music as if writing code, to create a nostalgic and adrenaline-fueled atmosphere that will accompany you on your dash.

Features

Totally Offline & Secure

Your privacy is our priority. CodeDash! is 100% offline. No data is collected, no internet connection required. The game uses an internal database (IndexedDB) to save your progress on your device and requires no special permissions.

Classic Gameplay, Modern Fun

  • Jump and Shoot: Face a vast pool of enemies, both on the ground and in the air, with simple and responsive controls.
  • Incredible Power-ups: Collect temporary boosts to unleash hell and permanent bonuses (for the current run) to become unstoppable.
  • Earn Currencies: Accumulate Bits and the precious digital Fruit to unlock new content.

Customize Your Adventure

Visit the brand-new in-game Shop to spend your earned currencies. Buy unique skins for the donkey, for its projectiles, and unlock permanent power-ups. With the Fruit, you can also acquire the incredible companions that will help you in your fight!

Challenges and Rewards

Complete Trophies and Badges to prove your skill and get exclusive rewards you won't find anywhere else.

Two Difficulty Levels

Normal Run: The classic CodeDash! experience.
Digital Rain Run: Wait in the menu for the alarm and the start of the digital rain. When it happens, start the run and fight 30% more enemies and stronger bosses! But the risk is worth the reward: when there's digital rain, there's also extra Digital Fruit!
